WHAT IS ARES?
  ARES = Actor Resolution & Entity Service
  - Identifies the OPERATORS behind counterfeit storefronts
  - Links domains by shared contact info (phone, email, handles)
  - Complements AEGE (which FINDS the counterfeit sites)

WHY THIS HACKATHON?
  - Showcases agentic development (Qoder)
  - Uses agent-native backend (Insforge)
  - Real production-grade code (not a toy)
  - Full stack in 6 hours

ARCHITECTURE:
  Frontend (React)
    ↓
  API (Express) with 5 routes
    ↓
  Services (6 orchestrated services)
    ↓
  Database (Postgres via Insforge)

BUILD TIMELINE:
  Phase 1 (Scaffold)    → 1 hour
  Phase 2 (Services)    → 2 hours
  Phase 3 (API Routes)  → 1.5 hours
  Phase 4 (UI + Demo)   → 1.5 hours
  ─────────────────────────────────
  TOTAL               → 6 hours
